# Monetization Models (Not Dependent on Users Failing)

This document focuses on revenue models that do not rely on users failing their goals. The goal is to monetize on value creation: structure, accountability, trust, and performance outcomes.

## 1) Subscription Tiers (Primary Path)

### A) Free (Onboarding + Trust Builder)
- Limited active protocols (e.g., 1)
- Basic evidence logs
- Basic witness support
- Limited analytics

### B) Pro (Prosumer)
- More active protocols (e.g., 3–10)
- Advanced templates and protocol builders
- Rich evidence history + export (PDF/CSV)
- Advanced analytics: streak trends, adherence rate, risk score
- Priority support

### C) Elite / Operator (High-intent users)
- Unlimited protocols
- Premium “enforcement tooling” (structured reviews, advanced reminders)
- Advanced integrations (calendar, email, WhatsApp)
- White-glove onboarding for serious users

## 2) Paid Witness / Coach Marketplace (High Leverage)

Offer a marketplace where users can pay for:
- Verified witnesses (fast approvals, structured accountability)
- Coaches (category specialists: fitness, learning, sales)

Pricing options:
- Per protocol (flat monthly)
- Per cycle (weekly)
- Revenue share on subscriptions

Key trust features needed later:
- Verified coach profiles
- SLA expectations (response time)
- Dispute escalation rules

## 3) Teams & Group Protocols (B2B2C)

Sell to:
- Friend groups
- Communities
- Work teams
- Cohort-based learning programs

Features:
- Team dashboards
- Group compliance scores
- Shared witness pools
- Role-based permissions (captain/admin)

Pricing:
- Per seat/month
- Per team/month
- Cohort fees (fixed for a group size)

## 4) Templates & Protocol Packs (One-Time Purchases)

Sell “protocol packs” built by experts:
- 30-day fitness pack
- 8-week learning sprint
- 12-week sales performance protocol

Includes:
- Prebuilt milestones
- Suggested proof types
- Recommended witness criteria

## 5) Integrations as Paid Add-ons

Paid integrations that improve adherence:
- Calendar scheduling + reminders
- Email/WhatsApp reminders
- Wearables (fitness)
- Learning platforms (course progress)

Revenue model:
- Add-on fee/month
- Higher tier unlock

## 6) Compliance & Reporting (B2B)

Organizations will pay for:
- Audit-friendly evidence trails
- Verified completion reporting
- Policy enforcement workflows

Examples:
- sales teams
- field teams
- training + certification programs

## 7) Affiliate & Partnerships (Optional)

Partnership revenue without pushing failure:
- Partner discounts for tools people use to succeed (courses, coaching, gym programs)
- Referral fees from partner services

## 8) “Success Insurance” Without Failure Incentives (Careful)

Only consider if phrased as value, not punishment:
- Users pay for premium accountability services that improve success odds.
- Avoid messaging that profits from penalties.

## 9) What to Implement Next vs Later

### Implement Next (Low engineering complexity, high ROI)
- Subscription tiers (feature gating + billing)
- Protocol templates (content + UI, little backend risk)

### Implement Later (Requires trust & ops)
- Coach/witness marketplace
- Team protocols
- B2B reporting

## 10) Recommended Monetization Strategy for Lockedin

- Start with **Pro subscription** for serious users who want structure + analytics.
- Add **templates** as a wedge to reduce friction and improve conversion.
- Once retention is strong, expand into **paid witnesses/coaches** and **teams**.
