# Pricing, Packaging & Monetization (Lockedin)

Date: 2026-06-07

## 1) What We Charge For (Principle)
Lockedin should monetize the “accountability infrastructure”, not user failure.
- People pay for: higher stake limits, better witness reliability, better tracking, better analytics, better support, and better trust/safety.
- Penalties remain an incentive mechanism. Revenue should not depend on users failing.

## 2) Core Monetization Streams (Beyond Penalties)
### A) Subscription tiers (primary)
- Feature unlocks (multi-vault, advanced analytics, premium templates, scheduling)
- Higher limits (stake caps, vault count, witness count)
- Priority support and faster dispute handling

### B) Transaction fee on funding (secondary)
- A small fee on Paystack “vault funding” (e.g. 1%–3% capped), clearly disclosed.
- Alternative: fixed convenience fee per funded vault.

### C) Witness network monetization
- “Verified Witness” program (paid + vetted): witnesses earn credits/reputation; users pay for access to higher-quality witnesses.
- Matching/priority queue: premium users get faster, higher-trust witness matching.

### D) Enterprise / Teams (B2B)
- Sell “Commitment Protocols” for teams (sales teams, students, remote teams).
- Admin dashboard + compliance logs + team leaderboards + coaching tooling.

### E) Partnerships / Affiliates
- Fitness/education creators: bundles, referral fees, co-branded challenges.
- BNPL/fintech partners later (careful with compliance wording and custody posture).

### F) Add-on products
- “Protocol Templates Pack” (creator marketplace later).
- “Accountability Coach” add-on (human coaching, group cohorts).
- “Evidence verification services” (for orgs/challenges).

## 3) Recommended MVP Packages
### Starter (Free)
For first-time users proving habit fit.
- 1 active vault
- Basic templates
- Community + leaderboard participation
- Standard witness matching (best-effort)
- Standard support

### Operator (Monthly subscription)
For consistent users who want multiple goals and better tooling.
- Up to 3 active vaults
- Higher stake cap
- Advanced templates + reminders/scheduling
- Better analytics (streaks, adherence trends, missed-check-in patterns)
- Priority witness matching
- Priority support

### Elite (Monthly subscription)
For high-stake, high-accountability users.
- Up to 10 active vaults
- Highest stake cap
- “Trusted Witness” matching
- Dispute tooling + evidence UX improvements
- Faster support SLA
- Optional “higher limits verification” gate (BVN or equivalent) if needed later

## 4) Pricing Notes (Nigeria-first practicality)
- Price monthly, not yearly, to reduce payment friction.
- Make the Operator tier affordable enough to be an impulse upgrade.
- Make Elite clearly about “limits + trust + support”, not “status”.

## 5) Revenue-Safety + Compliance Guardrails
- Always disclose any transaction fee before Paystack checkout.
- Keep a strict “non-custodial” stance in product language.
- Keep BVN optional and only introduce for higher limits if fraud/chargebacks force it.

## 6) Metrics to Validate Pricing
- Free → paid conversion rate
- % of paid users funding 2+ vaults per month
- Time-to-first-funded-vault
- Support load per 1,000 users
- Chargeback/dispute rate
